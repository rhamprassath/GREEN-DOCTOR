# 🗺️ Green Doctor: Future Roadmap

To move Green Doctor from a pilot project to a comprehensive tool for farmers, here are the proposed future features categorized by their impact.

## 🤖 1. AI & Core Technology Upgrades
*   **Offline Detection (On-Device AI)**: Convert the current models to TensorFlow Lite (.tflite) to allow detection without internet. Crucial for deep rural areas.
*   **Pest Identification**: Expand the AI to detect insects, larvae, and pests (e.g., Fall Armyworm), not just diseases.
*   **Multi-Leaf Batch Mode**: Allow scanning multiple leaves in one go to assess the overall health of a crop area.
*   **Dynamic Expert Loading**: Automatically download new "experts" (models) for specific seasonal crops (e.g., Sugarcane or Sesame).

## 📊 2. Farmer Utility & Support
*   **Weather-Based Risk Alerts**: Integrated weather forecasts that send alerts like: *"High humidity tomorrow - increased risk of Paddy Blast. Apply organic neem spray now."*
*   **Soil Health Integration**: A "File Upload" section where farmers can upload their Government Soil Health Card to get customized fertilizer recommendations.
*   **Voice Search / Voice UI**: An "Ask Green Doctor" feature where farmers can talk to the app in Tamil to find remedies instead of typing.
*   **Market Price Tracker (Mandi Prices)**: Real-time price tracking for crops in local markets (Karaikudi, Puduvayal, etc.).

## 👥 3. Community & Expert Connection
*   **Expert Hotline**: A direct WhatsApp or Call button connecting farmers to agriculture professors at ACGCET or local government agronomists.
*   **Disease Heatmap**: A map showing recent disease outbreaks in the region to alert neighboring farmers to take preventive measures.
*   **Success Stories**: A community section where Puduvayal farmers can share photos of their healthy crops and successful organic treatments.

## 💊 4. Digital Treatment Shop
*   **Remedy Locator**: A map showing the nearest certified shops or Cooperatives where farmers can buy the suggested organic/chemical treatments.
*   **Dosage Calculator**: A tool to calculate exactly how much medicine/fertilizer is needed based on the farm area (in Acres/Cents).

---
> [!NOTE]
> These features aim to make Green Doctor a "Daily Companion" for farmers, rather than just a diagnostic tool.
